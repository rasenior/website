3 How do we even measure colour?

  • it’s hard to measure colour in a way that is intuitively meaningful to humans
  • there are many different components to how we see things, and how we value what we see
  • and all of this is going to be very subjective, and probably varies between regions and cultures
  • it’s also hard to visualise colour, because standard ways of measuring colour are multidimensional
